About me

I am a 5 generation homestead owner from Colorado and currently a new breeder. I have always owned and loved dogs and horses as they have always been my passion, but it wasn't until the later chapter of my life did I decide to raise and breed French Bulldogs.

I make sure that all my dogs are adopted out to good homes with a $500.00 USD deposit to hold till they're of age at 8 weeks old and weaned. They're always vet checked and first shots ready before they're allowed to leave to their forever homes.

Please contact me if you're interested in one of my puppies, you can contact me through the Contact Us page or my facebook page.
